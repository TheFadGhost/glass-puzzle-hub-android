# V2 Puzzle Modes And High Refresh Research Plan

## Main Question

What should Glass Puzzle Hub v2 implement for high-refresh Android support, Shikaku/Sudoku modes, and future single-player game expansion?

## Subtopics

- Android high-refresh support: APIs, limits, and implementation implications.
- Shikaku variants: classic rules, grid-size/grid-shape modes, and blocked-cell variant legitimacy.
- Sudoku variants: which modes are established and which can be staged safely.
- Future game recommendations: single-player logic games with established rules and mobile-friendly input.

## Synthesis Target

Turn research into v2 implementation decisions: high-refresh window preference, playable Shikaku modes, honest Sudoku roadmap modes, and three future game cards.
